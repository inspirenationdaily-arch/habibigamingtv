PrivacyPolicy

Last updated: March 2026

HabibiGamingTV is a CS2 skin ranking and price guide site. We do not sell skins, process payments or collect sensitive personal data. This policy explains what limited data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

HabibiGamingTV does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address or payment details.

We may collect the following anonymous data automatically when you visit our site:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search query
  • General geographic location (country/region only)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This data is collected in aggregate and cannot be used to identify you personally.

How We Use Your Data

Any data we collect is used solely to improve the HabibiGamingTV website experience. Specifically we use it to:

  • Understand which pages and guides are most useful to our visitors
  • Improve site performance and loading speed
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand our audience demographics at a high level

We do not sell, rent or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Steam Market API

Our Marketplace and Top 10 pages fetch live skin prices from the Steam Community Market API via corsproxy.io. These requests are made from your browser and do not pass through our servers. No personal data is sent to Steam or corsproxy.io as part of this process.

Steam is operated by Valve Corporation. HabibiGamingTV is not affiliated with Valve Corporation in any way.

Third Party Links

Our site contains links to third-party websites including Steam Community Market, Twitch, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, X and other platforms. When you click these links you leave HabibiGamingTV and are subject to the privacy policies of those third-party sites.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of any third-party websites.

Cookies

HabibiGamingTV may use basic cookies to remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. We do not use cookies for advertising tracking or to sell data to third parties.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies will not prevent you from using HabibiGamingTV.
